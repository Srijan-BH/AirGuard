Understanding the Air Quality Index

AirGuard calculates the official US EPA 0-500 Standard. It works by measuring six major pollutants. Whichever pollutant has the most severe concentration becomes the driving AQI score for that hour.

PM2.5 & PM10

Microscopic particulate matter (dust, smoke). PM2.5 is so small it enters the bloodstream directly from the lungs.

Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂)

A highly reactive gas primarily emitted by vehicle exhaust and power plants. Causes severe respiratory issues.

Carbon Monoxide (CO)

An odorless, colorless gas produced by incomplete combustion. Reduces oxygen delivery to the body's organs.
